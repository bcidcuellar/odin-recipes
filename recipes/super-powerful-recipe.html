Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot - Wild Pasta

This wild pasta dish is inspired by the adventurous and flavorful world of Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot. It combines the bold flavors of wild game with a variety of fresh vegetables, creating a dish that's as exciting and satisfying as a battle against powerful foes. Enjoy this unique culinary journey!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of wild game meat(such as venison or wild boar), thinly sliced.
  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine works well)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Sensu Bean Sauce (soy sauce with a dash of hot sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon Goku's Grind (a mix of ground sesame seeds and seaweed)
  • 1 teaspoon Capsule Corp Crystals (crushed red pepper flakes)
  • 1 Dragon Ball-sized knob of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons Vegeta's Vinegar (rice vinegar)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Ki-infused water for boiling pasta (just for fun!)

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta in Ki-infused water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced wild game meat and cook until browned. Remove the meat from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add a bit more olive oil if needed. Saut√© the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger until softened and aromatic.
  4. Add the sliced bell pepper to the skillet and cook until slightly tender.
  5. Toss in the cherry tomatoes and sprinkle with Capsule Corp Crystals. Cook until they start to release their juices.
  6. Return the cooked wild game meat to the skillet and mix well with the vegetables.
  7. Season the mixture with dried oregano, dried basil, Sensu Bean Sauce, and Vegeta's Vinegar. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  8. Add the cooked pasta and fresh spinach to the skillet. Sprinkle Goku's Grind over the mixture. Toss everything together until the spinach wilts and the pasta is coated evenly with the flavorful sauce.
  9. Serve the Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot - Wild Pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of additional Goku's Grind for extra power!
